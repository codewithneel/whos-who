Welcome to Who's Who

How to Play:

  • Select your preferred music genre in the game settings.
  • You will be shown an image of a random artist from the chosen genre.
  • Choose the correct artist's name from the provided options.
  • If you guess correctly, another artist will appear for you to identify.
  • The game ends when you make an incorrect guess.
  • Your final score is the number of consecutive correct guesses.
